ATHLE FIT

Brand Identity

Client Brief

A logo is a central part of any business’s branding, as it’s usually the first point of contact for most potential customers. A logo is instantly identifiable and ensures that when customers look at it, they’ll connect with the brand.

That’s why it’s vital to make sure that it truly represents the brand and can connect with consumers when you make a logo.

Business Name: Athlefit for fitness & Leisure Solutions

Logo Name: Athlefit

Tagline/Slogan: Athlefit for fitness & Leisure Solutions Brand

Attributes:

  • Simple but Unique
  • Relevant
  • Memorable
  • Timeless
  • Versatile

Main Two Things To Communicate Through Our Logo Design:
#1 - Specializing in Fitness, Health & Wellness, Sport, & Leisure
#2 - Modern, simple, upscale but approachable

Note: We encourage designers to use the "Golden Ratio".
