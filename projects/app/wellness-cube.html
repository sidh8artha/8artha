Wellness Cube

by Neurotech Desgins Pvt Ltd

Mobile Application

Employeer Introduction

Company Overview

The wellness cube is designed & developed as a mediation device earlier. As the Covid-19 pandemic arrived in late 2019, the Indian household saw massive changes. The urgency of regular health checkups and quick diagnosis of the disease is preferred now a day. Features like pulse monitoring, blood pressure monitoring, oxygen level monitoring are adopted in newer version and thus we have to decided to new application.

Device Hardware

Wellness Cube is a device that helps you to monitor your heart rate, and oxygen level. It also measures the body temperature. User-friendly design and shape make the product worthy for each household. It also includes vibrational meditation features like wellness activities including guided sessions, breathing exercises, and relaxing music streaming.

Background: India & Health Care

The Problem

Someone easily saw the two nodes of Indian health care service. One with everything reachable counting from a blood test to complex cardiac surgery. while others are far away from the simple medical store accessibility. India has quite progressive in the public as well as private medical facilities. But as we go deeper one problem we came across is the mental health of Indians. The majority of Indians are not aware of mental health and its long time effects.

The recent covid-19 outburst pushed Indian society into self-isolation and health conscious state. Peoples are likely to take notes about their physical as well as mental health. A pulse oximeter, blood pressure monitor, blood sugar indicator, thermometer and becomes necessities of household items. But not everyone can afford them.

Higher stress lever, more anxious moods, anger management, and deprived sleep are becoming more common to us. No doubt meditation and yoga have been there for centuries for our help. People are indeed in need of simple and best methods to connect mind and body, either spiritually or scientifically.

The Solution

Amid of covid-19 breakout, an urgent need for small, efficient, and budget-friendly medical devices. The device should be easy to handle and will encourage the user for physical as well as mental health. The following enlisted features are a few which will help users manage their work-life balance in a perfect way.

A medical device should be able provide information about health parameters like a blood oximeter, heart rate monitor, body temperature, blood sugar level, and blood pressure.

A complete wellness app that should let the user be aware of physical and mental health. physical parameters like calories burnout count, the water level in the body, medicine time, their exercise session including yoga, and breathing exercise. The app should help users by means of guided meditation sessions, spiritual guidance, mentor consultation, and a large wellness community for self-esteem.
